Safety Resource Hub

Empowering citizens with knowledge and quick access to emergency services.

🛡️ Women's Safety (Kavalan)

  • Download the official Kavalan SOS App.
  • Share your live location with trusted contacts during travel.
  • Call 1091 for the dedicated Women's Helpline anytime.

💻 Cyber Fraud Prevention

  • Never share OTPs, PINs, or CVV with anyone.
  • Report cyber crimes immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
  • Verify links before clicking; police will never ask for money via UPI.

🚦 Road Safety Rules

  • Always wear helmets (both rider and pillion).
  • Avoid drunk driving; it ruins lives and incurs heavy penalties.
  • Give way to Ambulances and Fire Engines immediately.

Emergency Action Numbers

Police: 100
Emergency: 112
Ambulance: 108

Available 24/7. Never hesitate to call in emergencies.
